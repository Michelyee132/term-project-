Frequently Asked Questions

Will there be a sale, for example on Black Friday?

Absolutely not because the price we are giving are the lowest. If we go lower, we won't make profit, these products are already on sale.

Will the products get instantly sent to my in-game account?

Yes, but if you are already in game, rejoin to retrieve your purchases by checking the in-game mail.

Will purchases go through if I never played the game yet?

Yes, be sure to check the in-game mail after completing beginner quests.

What is your return policy?

What ever is purchased, can never be refunded. So think before you buy.

When will bundles and premium passes update prior to the newest game versions?

We will temporarily suspend this website when the game maintenance starts and will be reopened, as well the bundles and premium passes updated when the game reopens.
